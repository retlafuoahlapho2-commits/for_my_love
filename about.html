Our Love Story Celebration

Just like any love story, ours began on an ordinary Monday afternoon I remember it clearly because it was presentation day. A friend nudged me to take pictures with his girlfriend since she was with her friends, so I joined him; in the soft, unexpected light of that moment our first photograph was taken. We were quietly cruising through the day, two hearts finding an easy rhythm, and nothing and no one will ever change what began there..
